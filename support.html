Common questions

  • How do I manage or cancel my subscription? Open the Settings app on your device → tap your name → Subscriptions → Aerglo. You can change or cancel there. Subscriptions are billed through your Apple account.
  • I subscribed but Pro isn't showing. Open Aerglo → Upgrade → tap “Restore Purchases.” If it still doesn't unlock, email us.
  • The forecast looks wrong for my location. Make sure location access is enabled (Settings → Aerglo → Location), or set your location manually in the app.
  • How do I turn alerts on or off? Manage notification permissions in Settings → Aerglo → Notifications, and configure custom alerts inside the app.
  • How is my data handled? See our Privacy Policy. No account required, no tracking.

Refunds

Purchases are handled by Apple. Refund requests are managed through Apple at reportaproblem.apple.com.
